I'm Kimberley Agius

Kimberley Agius is a young visual artist from Malta. She holds a Diploma in Design and Foundation Studies and is currently reading for her Bachelor of Fine Arts in Digital Arts at the University of Malta. Throughout her studies and work, her aim has always been to create compelling and effective design solutions, across all media. Her work consists mainly of graphic design and sound design.

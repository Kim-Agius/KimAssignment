Barumbara Collective

Short film exhibited as part of the exhibition Verbi:Mill-Bieb 'il Gewwa at the Valletta Contemporary. The film was inspired by one of Loranne Vella's short stories 'Saff Saff'. The project was done with the help of other colleagues (Monique Casha, Maria-Elena Galea, Jean Paul Grech) as part of the Bachelor of Fine Arts in Digital Art course.
